Donate

Donate to Change Our World Today

Whether you choose to make a general donation, give to a specific need, or contribute to our student sponsorship program, we are committed to keeping you informed about how your support is making a difference.

We aim to keep our administrative costs low so your gift makes as much impact as possible –

96 cents of every $ you give goes directly to the Sega Girls School

It’s Easy to Donate

Online

You can donate online using your credit card or through PayPal. Just click on the “Donate” button below, complete the payment form, and submit.

By Mail

To donate by check, please print and complete the donation form (link)  and mail with your check to: Nurturing Minds, PO Box 144, Valley Forge, PA 19481 USA.

Credit Card Donation

To make a credit card donation, please print and complete the donation form (link to donation form) and mail to: Nurturing Minds, PO Box 144, Valley Forge, PA 19481 USA.

Stock Donation

Stock donations can be transferred to our Vanguard brokerage account:

Account Name: Nurturing Minds, Inc

Account Number: 75137837

Direct Transfer Code: 0062

Please let us know about your stock donation, so that we can send your tax acknowledgement appropriately: via e-mail or call 617-969-1950.

What Is Our Current Fundraising Focus?

Student Sponsorship Program

Supporting the student sponsorship program  (Link to student sponsorship/How the student sponsorship program works) will give you the opportunity to connect with and follow the progress of a Sega student. You can have first-hand experience of how your contribution is making a difference.

Furnishing The Current Campus Construction

Thanks to a USAID challenge grant of $1 million, we are currently in the final phase of construction of the Sega Girls School campus. We are now raising funds to furnish this construction:

  • $10 for a mosquito net
  • $20 for one set of sheets and blankets
  • $50 for a student desk and chair
  • $150 for a bed
  • $250 for a dormitory cabinet
  • $500 for a classroom cabinet
  • $1,000 for a computer

 Solar Panels

We are raising funds for solar panels and batteries for the new construction, so that the entire Sega Girls School campus can operate on solar energy. The total cost of the solar equipment for the six new buildings is $190,000, roughly $30,000 for each building.

If you are interested in donating solar equipment, or making a significant donation towards the purchase of solar equipment contact us via e-mail or call 617-969-1950.
